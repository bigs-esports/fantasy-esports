Since 2017 Liqcorice has led this team as the captain, and the MVP of this team his impact on the game knows no bounds. With a legendary stat line season after season, he alwasy stood out as a prime leader and astounding top laner. Nisqy

Middle Lane

Nisqy was born in Morocco, where he began playing League of Legends at an early age. He competed in tournaments both locally and regionally in his home and quickly expanded to outlying cities. Later, he also competed in tournaments in Germany, Norway, and the United States. Drafted in 2018 Nisqy has been a a leading player in the Middle Lane for two seasons. He stands tall ammong nationally ranked players and is a force to be reckoned with.

Zven

Bottom Lane

Zven is a professional Leage of Legends player with Cloud9. Zven garnered national attention for his impressive play at Davidson College. He was drafted in 2017 by the Golden Guardians and eventually developed into one of Esports top players with his stellar shooting skills. After garnering Most Valuable Player honors and helping the team win the LCS championship in 2018, Zven led the team to a league-record 15 wins during the 202 season. In May 2016, he became the first person to be named MVP by unanimous vote in Leaguse history, and one of only 11 players to win the MVP award two years in a row.

Vulcan

Support

Vulcan was a high school Esports star and after playing for only one season at the University of Texas, he was chosen second overall in the 2019 draft by Cloud9 and went on the recieve Rookie of the Year. Vulcan went on to become a two-time LCS champion for the organization, which became the the catalyst to his success, and in 2020 he was voted the league's Most Valuable Player.
